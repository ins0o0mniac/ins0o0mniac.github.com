Charities to support <3

World Cancer Research Fund

World Cancer Research Fund is a leading cancer prevention charity, funding worldwide scientific research into the links between diet, weight, physical activity, cancer prevention and survival. Translating the findings into practical, easy-to-use information to help everyone reduce their risk of cancer. But our life-saving work can’t stop there, cancer rates continue to rise and as we receive no government funding, this innovative scientific research work is only possible through the generosity of people like you, especially from gifts in wills. Having a current will is the best way to provide for your family and friends and we understand they must come first. But we urge you to join others to help prevent cancer and save lives by including a gift to World Cancer Research Fund when you write or next update your will. Your gift would enable us to continue helping more people live healthier cancer-free lives. What better legacy to leave your loved ones and future generations than the power to prevent cancer.  

CRISIS TEXT LINE

When people need to talk to a crisis counselor ASAP, Crisis Text Line offers free 24/7 support. Anyone can text 741741 from anywhere in the US to be connected to a trained crisis counselor, which is an especially helpful resource for people struggling with anxiety and depression during the COVID-19 pandemic.

 World Central Kitchen

World Central Kitchen (WCK) has served over 25 million meals to the hungry since 2010, and now it’s tackling emergency food relief during a global pandemic. Working in dozens of cities, it provides over 250,000 fresh meals each day to those without access to food—and it’s doing this by working with restaurants directly to help them keep their doors open while also nourishing people in need during COVID-19.
